What We Do

Four services.
One specialist.

We cover everything a fire door needs from first inspection through to long term maintenance. One qualified team, one point of contact, and documentation that holds up to scrutiny at every stage.

01
Inspect

UK Fire Door Training qualified inspectors delivering thorough, image backed reports via Digital Door Data systems. Clear findings, practical recommendations and cloud access at any time.

02
Install

Our principle is always remediate before replace. Where it is safe and compliant to repair, we will. Where new installation is needed, our UK Fire Door Training accredited team of master carpenters is ready.

03
Certify

Every completed job results in a certificate for your fire door or door set. Issued the same day, digitally, and held on record so your audit trail is always complete and accessible.

04
Maintain

Fire door compliance is an ongoing responsibility. We offer maintenance packages from 2 to 10 years so you can hand that responsibility to a qualified team and focus on everything else.

Why Us

The competent team
you have been
looking for.

The Regulatory Reform (Fire Safety) Order 2005, strengthened by the Fire Safety Act 2021 and the Fire Safety (England) Regulations 2022, requires you, as the responsible person, to appoint a competent contractor for any work on your fire doors. We take that responsibility seriously so you do not have to. Approachable, affordable and always straight with you.

No scare tactics

We explain the compliance process in plain terms, help you understand what is needed and why, and keep you informed at every stage. No unnecessary upselling.

Digital Door Data Systems

Our inspection reports go beyond industry standards. Image backed, detailed and stored in the cloud so your documentation is ready whenever you or an auditor needs it.

Remediate before replace

We understand the cost pressure on landlords and managing agents. Wherever it is safe and compliant, we repair first. You get the outcome you need without unnecessary expense.
